---
title: "I"
date: 2015-12-01
---

I
just want to be
a loving whisper 
between your lips... 
I
just want to see
my name and yours
under a red-leaved tree... 
I
just want to hear
your laugh like a beacon, 
my light on this sea... 
I
just want to feel
your hands healing 
under my skin. 
I
just want to meet
your past, your future, 
your feelings, your fears. 
I
just tried to make poetry,
I just tried to write down
this repetitive dream... 
I just tried to stop
what I'm starting to feel, 
I just tried to bring you
a little closer to me...
